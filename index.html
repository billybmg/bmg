AboutUs

With over 10 years’ experience, Billy’s philosophy is to “provide a great service by treating each customer with the utmost respect, professionalism and the highest quality workmanship. Our commitment to excellence is what separates us from the rest. When you’re with us, you’re in good hands.”

One sunny September morning, BMG Aluminium & Security was born in the beautiful coastal town of Gonubie, East London. Which started off as one man’s dream to do what he loves, has now, a few years down the line, become an established business - thanks to you, our customers.

With the exciting move from East London to the friendly city of Port Elizabeth in 2017, BMG Aluminium & Security aims to become a trusted name when thinking of purchasing blinds, flooring, aluminium & security.
